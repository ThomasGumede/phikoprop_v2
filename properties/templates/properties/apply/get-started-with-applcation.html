How It works?

Apply For Accommodation with NPI in 3 easy steps.

1

Create your Account

If you don't already have an account with us, consider creating one or login.

2

Add Application

Login & choose property you wish to stay in below and begin your application. You can apply online or download application form and fill it. Please ready our terms of application, fees and required documents for your application to be considered.

3

Submit Application And Payment

Upload all required documents and submit your online application. You can also email the application form to apply@npi.co.za and attach all the required documents.

If you experience a glitch unexpectedly, simply send a screenshot of the trouble you've run into to support@npi.co.za and we'll sort it out.

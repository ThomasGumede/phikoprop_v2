about us

Building a Better Future in Real Estate

Ndwandwa Property Investments (Pty) Ltd is a real-estate company focused on providing high-end, modern rental accommodations for students, learners, and first-time workers, with an emphasis on security and efficient service. With a base in the Eastern Cape (Mthatha), the company aims to expand its operations to other provinces, particularly in rural areas.


Their mission is to promote black-owned real estate enterprises through partnerships, create sustainable growth, and offer well-maintained, multicultural accommodation facilities. The company envisions expanding to meet increasing accommodation demand by identifying and developing vacant sites in rural areas.
